Here's my implementaion for Fancy Blog App using Angular Material & Bootstrap v5.1.3

  • Posts page containes list of post cards with front side pagenation bar, on pressing details button you will see the post with all of it's comments below
  • Albums page containes list of albums cards just like the posts page, on pressing details you will navigate to album's detail page which containes album's info & all of it's photos below
  • Add Post page you can add post title and content then press submit to create.
